Academic Programs & Training

Cambridge Hospital has a long-standing tradition of fostering medical excellence through robust academic initiatives. We believe that a strong academic foundation is essential for advancing healthcare and providing exceptional patient outcomes.

Undergraduate & Internship Programs

We offer structured clinical training programs for undergraduate medical and nursing students. Our internship programs provide hands-on experience across key departments including Emergency, Surgery, Pediatrics, and Internal Medicine under the supervision of experienced faculty.

Residency & Fellowship

Cambridge Hospital offers postgraduate residency programs in collaboration with top medical universities. Specialized fellowship programs in fields such as Cardiology, Oncology, and Critical Care Medicine are also available for advanced clinical training.

Continuing Medical Education (CME)

Our CME programs ensure that doctors, nurses, and technicians stay updated with the latest medical advancements. Regular workshops, seminars, and certification courses are organized throughout the year.

Research & Publications

Our doctors and researchers are actively involved in clinical and academic research. We publish in reputed medical journals and support staff participation in national and international conferences.

Academic Collaborations

We have academic tie-ups with renowned institutions including Cambridge University School of Medicine, All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), and other global universities to facilitate student exchange, research, and dual certification.
