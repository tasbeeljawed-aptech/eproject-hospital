About Cambridge Hospital

Founded in 1928 by Dr. John Cambridge, our hospital has been a beacon of hope and healing for nearly a century. We specialize in handling critical and complex medical cases with advanced technology and highly skilled doctors. Our commitment to patient care and medical innovation has earned us numerous accolades and the trust of the community.

Our Achievements

  • Successfully treated over 500,000 patients across various specialties.
  • First hospital in the region to implement robotic surgery techniques.
  • Accredited by national and international medical organizations.
  • Established advanced emergency response and trauma care units.
